Abstraction:

  • Hiding internal implementation and just highlight the set of services is the concept of abstraction .
  • By using abstract class and interfaces we can achieve abstraction .
  • The main advantages of abstraction are :
    • We are not highlighting our internal implementation, hence we can achieve security .
    • Without effecting end-user we can perform any kind of changes in our internal design .
    • It improves maintainability and modularity of the application .
