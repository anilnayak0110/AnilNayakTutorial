Combination Of Try Catch and Finally

  • We can take try-catch-finally inside try, catch and finally blocks i.e nesting of try catch and finally is possible .
  • Whenever we are taking try compulsory we have to write either catch or finally i.e, try without catch or finally is invalid .
  • Whenever we are writing catch compulsory try block should be required i.e, catch without try is always invalid .
  • Whenever we are writing finally block compulsory try block should be required i.e, finally without try is always invalid .
  • In try-catch-finally order is important .
  • For try-catch and finally blocks curly braces are mandatory .
  • Once we entered into the try block without executing finally block we can not go out .
  • If the control won't entered into try block, then finally block won't be executed .
