HAS-A-Relationship:

  • It is also known as Composition or Aggregation .
  • There is no specific keyword to implement HAS-A relationship. But, mostly we can use new operator .
  • The advantage is reusability .
  • The main advantages of HAS-A-Relationship is reusability. But the main advantages is it increases dependency between the components and it effects maintainability .

Composition vs Aggregation:

Composition :

  • Without existing container object if there is no chance of existing contained objects, then these container and contained objects are strongly associated and this strong association is called as composition .
Example: A university consists of several departments. Without existing University there is no chance of existing Department . Hence University and Department objects are strongly associated and this relationship is nothing but Composition .

Aggregation :

  • Without existing of Container object there may be a chance of existing Contained Objects, then Container and Contained objects are weekly associated, which is called Aggregation.
Example: Within a University there are several Professors . Without existing of Department Object there may be a chance of existing of Professors . Hence Department and Professors Objects are weakly associated, which is nothing but Aggregation .
Note: In composition, Objects are strongly associated, where as in Aggregation, Objects are weakly associated .
